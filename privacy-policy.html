N° 01

Privacy Policy

JVK Technologies is committed to protecting the privacy of scholars, researchers, students, authors, creators and small businesses who use our research, publication, patent, copyright, thesis, e-publishing, web hosting and technical training services.

This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, who has access to it, and the choices you have. By engaging JVK Technologies for services or contacting us through this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The business aims to follow applicable Indian data protection rules, including the IT Act 2000 and Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, and relevant privacy obligations for international clients where applicable.

N° 02

Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you enquire about services, engage us for work, or correspond with our team. This may include:

  • Your name, email address and phone number
  • Subject area, deadline and current stage of your work
  • Drafts, manuscripts, invention notes, academic files and supporting documents you share
  • Billing and payment information required for service agreements
  • Communication history — emails, messages and meeting notes

We may also collect limited technical information when you visit this website, such as your browser type, referring page and approximate region. We do not use third-party advertising trackers on this site.

N° 03

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for basic functionality. We do not use advertising, tracking or third-party analytics cookies.

If we add non-essential cookies in the future (for example, to remember preferences or measure site performance), we will update this policy and provide a way for you to opt out. You can also control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

For reference, browsers typically allow you to refuse third-party cookies, delete existing cookies, and be notified when a cookie is being set. Disabling cookies may affect some website features but will not prevent you from contacting us by email or phone.

N° 04

Data Usage

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:

  • To scope, prepare, review and deliver the agreed service
  • To respond to enquiries and provide the next practical step
  • To maintain records of work, milestones and review discussions
  • To meet legal, regulatory or contractual obligations
  • To improve our internal processes and service quality

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information or work material. Research drafts, invention notes, academic files and client details are treated as confidential working material and shared only with team members directly involved in the engagement.

N° 05

User Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the following rights over your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal or contractual retention obligations
  • Restriction — request that we limit the use of your information in certain circumstances
  • Portability — receive your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for processing at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, write to admin@jvktech.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days.

N° 06

Data Protection & Retention

JVK Technologies handles client information for service delivery and communication. We aim to follow applicable Indian data protection rules, including the IT Act 2000 and Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, and relevant privacy obligations for international clients where applicable.

Client material is retained for as long as necessary to provide the agreed service and meet legal or contractual obligations. After the completion of an engagement, working material may be securely archived for a limited period to allow for follow-up queries, and then deleted unless retention is required by law.

While we take reasonable measures to protect client information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect your material using access controls, confidentiality practices and secure communication channels.

N° 07

Terms

  • Acceptance of terms — By accessing this website or engaging JVK Technologies for services, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old or have legal authority to agree to these terms.
  • Services and delivery — Services, deliverables, timelines and fees are agreed before work begins. Clients must provide accurate and timely information. Reasonable revisions may be included according to the agreed scope; additional revisions can require extra fees.
  • Intellectual property — JVK Technologies retains ownership of proprietary methods and materials. Client-specific deliverables belong to the client after full payment unless a written agreement states otherwise. Clients must ensure that materials they provide do not infringe third-party rights.
  • Limitation of liability — JVK Technologies is not responsible for delays or outcomes controlled by third parties, including journals, patent offices or admission bodies. Liability is limited to the amount paid for the specific service, except where liability cannot be excluded under law.
  • Disputes and governing law — Disputes are governed by Indian law. Arbitration, where required, will be handled in Gudivada, Andhra Pradesh, India under applicable arbitration law.
