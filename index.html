Common questions

Before you contact us.

What should I send first?

A short note with your service requirement, subject area, deadline and any draft or supporting files you already have.

Do you handle confidential material?

Yes. Research drafts, invention notes, academic files and client details are treated as private working material.

Can you help if my work is still early?

Yes. Early-stage support can include topic direction, document structure, planning, checklists and next-step guidance.

How do we decide the scope?

We review your requirement, current material, timeline and expected output before suggesting the next practical step.
